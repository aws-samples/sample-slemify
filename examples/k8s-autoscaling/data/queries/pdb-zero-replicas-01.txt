we have a deployment that KEDA scales to 0 replicas when idle. but we also have a PDB with minAvailable: 1 on it. when KEDA tries to scale to 0, the PDB blocks the last pod from being evicted. the pod just sits there. is there a way to make PDB and scale-to-zero work together?

```yaml
apiVersion: policy/v1
kind: PodDisruptionBudget
metadata:
  name: worker-pdb
spec:
  minAvailable: 1
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: worker
```
