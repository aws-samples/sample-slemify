our batch processing pods have terminationGracePeriodSeconds: 300 for checkpointing to S3. but during spot interruptions the pods get SIGKILL'd almost immediately. karpenter logs show "Forceful Termination" even though we set terminationGracePeriod: 10m on the NodePool.

here's the relevant NodePool config:
```yaml
spec:
  template:
    spec:
      terminationGracePeriod: 10m
      requirements:
        - key: karpenter.sh/capacity-type
          operator: In
          values: ["spot"]
```

and the pod spec:
```yaml
terminationGracePeriodSeconds: 300
lifecycle:
  preStop:
    exec:
      command: ["/bin/sh", "-c", "python checkpoint.py && sleep 120"]
```

the preStop hook takes about 2 minutes to complete the checkpoint. but AWS only gives a 2-minute spot warning. is there a way to get more time? an LLM said we can configure the spot warning period.
