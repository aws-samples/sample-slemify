we have a 3-node etcd cluster (not the k8s etcd, our own) with maxUnavailable: 2 in the PDB. during a karpenter node rotation, 2 etcd pods went down and we lost quorum. data writes failed for 4 minutes.

```yaml
apiVersion: policy/v1
kind: PodDisruptionBudget
metadata:
  name: etcd-pdb
spec:
  maxUnavailable: 2
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: etcd-cluster
```

an LLM said maxUnavailable 2 leaves 1 node running which is enough. but etcd needs majority quorum (2 of 3). what should maxUnavailable be?
